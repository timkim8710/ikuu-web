The Onboarding Sequence

Follow this sequential registration pipeline upon receipt of an official calling letter:

Step 1: Document Clearance

Present your original KCPE result slip, NEMIS registration configuration profile, and primary institution leaving certificates to the admissions desk.

Step 2: Medical Examination

Submit the filled medical history verification document signed and stamped by a registered government health officer.

Step 3: Uniform & Boarding Assignment

Complete kit checks at the institutional stores and obtain house block desk numbers from the boarding master.
